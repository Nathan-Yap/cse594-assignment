MTurk Research Study

Vehicle Description Labelling Study

You will be shown images of vehicles and asked to identify which description best matches each image. The study takes approximately 5–10 minutes.

Instructions

1
Click Begin Study to receive your unique survey code.
2
Copy and paste your survey code back into the MTurk HIT field before proceeding.
3
You will be shown 10 vehicle images, each with several candidate descriptions.
4
Select the description that best matches the vehicle, or choose "Not listed" if none apply.
5
Complete a short feedback form at the end.
